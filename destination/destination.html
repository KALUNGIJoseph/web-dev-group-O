Things to do in Queen Elizabeth National Park

Queen Elizabeth National Park is a popular wildlife destination in western Uganda, known for its diverse ecosystems, from savannah to rainforests, and abundant wildlife, including the famous tree-climbing lions. Visitors can go on game drives, take boat cruises on the Kazinga Channel, go chimpanzee trekking, and see a wide variety of bird species.
